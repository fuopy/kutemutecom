Projects

AMIGO-600: VRC Music Creation

Make music with your friends in VRChat all at the same time using this free, open source world prefab! Simply find web videos that you enjoy, and chop up some small samples from them. After that, put the samples on cartridges to give to your friends. Then everyone can work together to sequence the final composition!

mimiuchi: Speech-To-Text-To-Speech

A free and open source tool that lets you control how you express yourself in realtime online communications.
